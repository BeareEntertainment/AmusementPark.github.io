Attractions

Discover the thrilling rides and attractions at Arusha Adventure Park:

Serengeti Safari Adventure

Experience a thrilling safari ride through our simulated Serengeti landscape. Encounter life-sized animatronic wildlife and enjoy a guided tour through the savannah.

Superhero City

Meet your favorite superheroes in our themed area. Enjoy superhero-themed rides, interactive shows, and photo opportunities with iconic characters.

Boardwalk Town

Feel the adrenaline rush on our wild mouse coaster. Explore other exciting rides and enjoy the nostalgic boardwalk atmosphere with games and food stalls.

Masaii Fields

Immerse yourself in the rich culture of the Maasai people. Experience traditional dances, cultural exhibits, and interactive activities in a beautifully themed area.

Aqua Adventure Oasis

Cool off at our world-class water park featuring thrilling water slides, wave pools, and relaxing lazy rivers. Perfect for a fun day in the sun!
