About Us

Arusha Adventure Park & Aqua Adventure Oasis is dedicated to providing a world-class entertainment experience for families and thrill-seekers alike. Our park features a variety of attractions, from thrilling rides to cultural exhibits, ensuring fun for all ages.

Founded in 2024, our park has quickly become a favorite destination in Tanzania, offering a range of exciting activities and events throughout the year.
