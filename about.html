About Us

Arusha Adventure Park & Aqua Adventure Oasis is dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences for visitors of all ages. Our park features a variety of thrilling attractions, including safari adventures, superhero encounters, and exciting rides. The Aqua Adventure Oasis offers a refreshing escape with its world-class water slides and pools.

Founded in 2020, our park has quickly become a favorite destination for families and thrill-seekers alike. Our mission is to deliver exceptional fun and adventure while maintaining the highest standards of safety and customer service.
